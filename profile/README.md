## 🌐 Who We Are

As artificial intelligence, data science, and digital technologies continue to reshape the life sciences industry, professional organizations must evolve beyond traditional operations and embrace modern, scalable, and intelligent ways of working.

The **CABS IT & Data Science (ITDS) Committee** was established with a bold vision: to build the technology, data, and AI foundation that will support the next decade of growth, innovation, and community impact across CABS.

---

## 🎯 Our Mission

Our mission extends far beyond IT support. We are working to create a fully connected, **AI-enabled ecosystem** that integrates:

- 👥 Membership management
- 📅 Events & community programs
- 🤝 Mentorship & internships
- 📣 Communications
- 📊 Analytics & leadership decision-making

…all powered by modern **digital infrastructure**, **data governance**, **workflow automation**, and **AI-powered solutions**.

We envision a future where CABS operates as a seamless, data-driven organization that can scale globally while delivering exceptional value to members.

## 🔭 Our 2035 Vision

Our long-term goal is ambitious: by **2035**, CABS will not simply be an organization that hosts events, but a **digitally integrated innovation platform** that connects talent, knowledge, mentorship, technology, and opportunity across the global life sciences community.

Through collaboration, experimentation, and continuous learning, we hope to cultivate the next generation of **AI-enabled scientists, entrepreneurs, and community leaders**.
